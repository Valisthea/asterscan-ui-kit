DM Sans is the Google Fonts substitute for the proprietary TT Turns face used in the AsterScan UIKit. The two are visually identical at common UI sizes; use DM Sans wherever the AsterScan source font is unavailable. JetBrains Mono is the canonical mono face for prices, addresses, and any tabular numeric content.
