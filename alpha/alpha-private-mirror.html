Private Mirror

Inferring signal from encrypted on-chain traffic.

Around 70-90% of Aster Chain traffic uses privacy mode — encrypted order payloads. Every other explorer treats those as black holes. Private Mirror reads the metadata that's still public (timing, size class, sender, validator) and infers aggregate flows.

  • Long/short bias gauge with confidence interval
  • Privacy whale heatmap (size-classed, anonymized)
  • Correlated address clusters via co-occurrence inference
